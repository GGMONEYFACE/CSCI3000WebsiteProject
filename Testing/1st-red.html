1st Black Belt Testing Requirements

Students must know and be able to recite all 12 Bible verses at the start of belt testing. Students must also know all previous testing requirements.

  1. 1. Bassai
  2. 2. Original One-Steps created by the student
  3. 3. Knife Defense as well as gun defense
  4. 4. Kick pad drills
  5. 5. All basic phase kicks, plus all phases of hook, back, and outside crescent kicks
  6. 6. Attacking techniques with and without partner
  7. 7. Free sparring against five people and then five people at once
  8. 8. Will have an oral exam, must turn in written essay
  9. 9. Must explain pressure point locations and uses
  10. 10. Break five boards with kicking-punching combination, and break five boards at once
