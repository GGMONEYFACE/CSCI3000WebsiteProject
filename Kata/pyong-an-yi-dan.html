Required for 6th Green and above.

  1. 1. Junbi stance. Look left and fold both fists at your right hip, ball and socket fashion with the left fist on top. Shift your left foot to the left into a back stance. Throw your left and right arms up at the same time, the left arm like an outside block and your right as a high block. Your arms form two parts of a square, and your left fist has palm out toward opponent.
  2. 2. As if grabbing your opponent, pull your left fist down and in to strike your right bicep while you strike your opponent with an uppercut with the right fist. Turn into a front stance.
  3. 3. Jab with your right fist as you pull your left foot back into a cat stance. Chamber your left fist.
  4. 4. Execute a side punch with the left fist while going into a horse stance, stepping forward with your left foot.
  5. 5. Look to your right. Fold both fists at your left hip, ball and socket fashion with the right fist on top. Throw your left and right arms up at the same time, the right arm like an outside block and your left as a high block. Your arms form two parts of a square, and your right fist has palm out toward opponent.
  6. 6. As if grabbing your opponent, pull your right fist down and in to strike your left bicep while you strike your opponent with an uppercut with the left fist. Turn into a front stance.
  7. 7. Jab with your left fist as you pull your right foot back into a cat stance. Chamber your right fist.
  8. 8. Execute a side punch with the right fist while going into a horse stance, stepping forward with your right foot.
  9. 9. Look over your right shoulder at opponent behind you. Slide your left foot up to your right foot, keeping your right side toward opponent and folding your fists ball and socket fashion on your right hip. Immediately raise your arms up in front of you, folding for bottom fist with your right hand. Strike opponent with a bottom fist and right side kick at the same time.
  10. 10. Immediately fold for a reinforced knife hand block, left hand, 180 degrees in the opposite direction. Land in a back stance as you execute the block.
  11. 11. Step forward and fold for and execute a reinforced knife hand block, right hand, back stance.
  12. 12. Step forward and fold for and execute a reinforced knife hand block, left hand, back stance.
  13. 13. Step forward and execute a spear hand, right hand, front stance. KIAI
  14. 14. Look to the right. Fold for a reinforced knife hand block, left hand, turning 270 degrees around to the left, execute the block, back stance.
  15. 15. Look to the right 45 degrees. Fold for and execute a knife hand block, right hand, back stance.
  16. 16. Look to the right (more than 45 degrees). Fold for and execute a knife hand block, left hand, back stance.
  17. 17. Look to the left 45 degrees. Fold for and execute and execute a knife hand block, left hand, back stance.
  18. 18. Look to the left 45 degrees. Fold for an outside block, right hand. Execute the block while stepping left 45 degrees, front stance. Chamber the left fist.
  19. 19. Jab with the right fist, followed immediately by a stepping front kick, right leg. Land in a front stance.
  20. 20. Reverse punch, left hand.
  21. 21. Fold for an outside block with your left arm. Execute the block, staying in the front stance.
  22. 22. Jab with the left fist, followed immediately by a stepping front kick, left leg. Land in a front stance.
  23. 23. Reverse punch, right hand.
  24. 24. While stepping forward with the right foot, fold for a reinforced outside block, right arm. Tje fold is fists knuckle to knuckle over the left hip. Land in a front stance, right foot forward for the reinforced outside block, right arm.
  25. 25. Look to the right 90 degrees. Fold for a low block, left arm. Turn 270 degrees (spinning around to the left), execute low block, left arm, front stance. Immediately execute a knife hand block, left arm, staying in the front stance.
  26. 26. Look to the right 45 degrees. Fold for a high block, right arm. Step to the right 45 degrees, right foot, execute the high block, right arm, back stance.
  27. 27. Look to the right (more than 45 degrees), fold for a low block, right arm. Step to the right with the right foot, low block, front stance. Immediately execute a knife hand block, right arm, staying in the front stance.
  28. 28. Look to the left 45 degrees, Fold for a high block, left arm. Step to the left 45 degrees, left foot, execute the high block, left arm, back stance.

Return to the Junbi stance.
