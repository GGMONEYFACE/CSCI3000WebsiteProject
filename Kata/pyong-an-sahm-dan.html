Required for 5th Green and above.

  1. 1. Junbi stance. Look to your left, fold for an outside block with your left hand while stepping to your left with your left foot. At the same time turn 90 degrees to your left to a back stance, executing an outside block with the left arm.
  2. 2. Slide your right foot next to your left foot, and as you do, strike to the groin with your right fist while leaving your left arm in the outside block position. Execute a double block, hands changing places twice.
  3. 3. Look over your right shoulder. Fold for an outside block with your right hand while stepping behind with your right foot. At the same time, turn 180 degrees to your right into a back stance, executing the outside block with the right arm.
  4. 4. Slide your left foot next to your right foot, and as you do, strike to the groin with your left fist while leaving your right arm in outside block position. Execute a double block, hands changing places twice.
  5. 5. Look to your left. Fold for a reinforced outside block with your left arm while you step to the left with your right foot. At the same time, turn 90 degrees to your left to a front stance, executing the reinforced outside block with your left arm.
  6. 6. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance, executing a spear-hand with your right hand to the throat.
  7. 7. Grab your opponent with your right hand, Execute a spinning bottom fist, left hand, then a stepping reverse punch with your right hand. KIAI
  8. 8. Turn 180 degrees around to the left, raising your left foot knee high. At the same time, both hands are raised head height and to each side as you turn. Slowly lower both fists to your hips as your left foot lowers and touches the floor. Take a small step forward with your left foot.
  9. 9. Execute a high inside crescent kick with the right leg. As your foot lands (in horse stance), turn your upper body and block with your upper right arm and shoulder, then execute a back fist - your right fist returns to your hip.
  10. 10. Execute a high inside crescent kick with the left leg. As your foot touches the floor, land in horse stance. Turn your upper body and block with your upper left arm and shoulder, Execute a back fist with the left hand, The left fist returns to the hip.
  11. 11. Execute another high inside crescent kick with the right leg. As your right foot lands in a horse stance, turn your upper body, blocking with the upper right arm and shoulder. Execute a back fist. The fist remains in a striking position as you turn your body into a front stance.
  12. 12. Execute a stepping center punch (left hand) into a front stance, left foot in the front. Your right foot moves forward and touches your left foot and then moves out to the right into a horse stance.
  13. 13. Turn 180 degrees to your left. Look over your left shoulder, executing a right hand punch over your left shoulder as you do an elbow strike with your left elbow.
  14. 14. Lunge to your right into a horse stance, executing a left hand punch over your right shoulder as you execute a right elbow strike with your right elbow. KIAI

Return to the Junbi stance.
