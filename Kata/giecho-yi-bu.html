Required for 9th Purple and above.

  1. 1. Junbi (ready) stance. Look to your left, fold with your left hand for a low block, stepping to your left with your left foot, turn 90 degrees to the left into a front stance, executing a down block with the left arm.
  2. 2. Execute a stepping front kick with your right foot, landing in a front stance, right foot forward, followed by a reverse punch with your right hand to the face.
  3. 3. Look over your right shoulder, fold for a down block with your right hand, step behind you with your right foot, spinning on your left foot and turning 180 degrees into a front stance, executing a down block with right arm.
  4. 4. Execute a stepping front kick with left foot, landing in a front stance, left foot forward, followed by a reverse punch with your left hand to the face.
  5. 5. Look to your left, fold with your left hand for a low block, stepping to your left with your left foot, turn 90 degrees to the left into a back stance, executing a down block, followed by a reverse punch with the right hand to the solar plexus, front stance.
  6. 6. Fold for inside block with the right arm, step forward with your right foot into a back stance and execute an inside block with the right arm, followed by a reverse punch with left hand to the face, front stance.
  7. 7. Fold for a left arm high block, step forward into a back stance with your left foot, executing the high block, followed by a reverse punch with right hand to the face, front stance.
  8. 8. Fold for an outside block with your right arm, step forward with your right foot into a back stance executing an outside block, followed by a re- verse punch with your left hand, front stance and KIAI.
  9. 9. Look to your right, fold with your left hand for a low block, spinning on your right foot and turning 270 degrees around to the left with your left foot into a front stance, left foot forward, executing a down block with left arm.
  10. 10. Execute a stepping front kick with right foot, landing in a front stance, right foot forward, followed by a reverse punch to the face with your right hand.
  11. 11. Look over your right shoulder, fold for a down block with your right hand, step behind you with your right foot, spinning on your left foot and turning 180 degrees into a front stance, executing a down block with right arm.
  12. 12. Execute a stepping front kick with left foot, landing in a front stance, left foot forward, followed by a reverse punch with your left hand to the face.
  13. 13. Look over left shoulder, fold with your left hand for a low block, step- ping to your left with your left foot, turn 90 degrees to the left into a back stance, executing a down block, followed by a reverse punch with the bold right hand to the solar plexus, front stance.
  14. 14. Fold for inside block with the right arm, step forward with your right foot into a back stance and execute an inside block with the right arm, followed by a reverse punch with left hand to the face, front stance.
  15. 15. Fold for a left arm high block, step forward into a back stance with your left foot, executing the high block, followed by a reverse punch with right hand to the face, front stance.
  16. 16. Fold for an outside block with your right arm, step forward with your right foot into a back stance executing an outside block, followed by a reverse punch with your left hand, front stance and KIAI.
  17. 17. Look to your left, fold with your left hand for a low block, spinning on your right foot and turning 270 degrees back and around with your left foot into a front stance, left foot forward, executing a down block with left arm.
  18. 18. Execute a stepping front kick with right foot, landing in a front stance, right foot forward, followed by a reverse punch with your right hand to the face.
  19. 19. Look over your right shoulder, fold for a down block with your right hand, step behind you with your right foot, spinning on your left foot and turning 180 degrees into a front stance, executing a down block with right arm.
  20. 20. Execute a stepping front kick with left foot, landing in a front stance, left foot forward, followed by a reverse punch with your left hand to the face.

Return to the Junbi stance.
