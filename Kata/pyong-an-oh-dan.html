Required for 2nd Red and above.

  1. 1. Junbi stance. Look to your left. Fold for an outside block with your left hand as you step 90 degrees to the left with your left foot. Execute the outside block in a back stance. With your right hand, punch across your body to your left.
  2. 2. Draw your right foot to your left foot as you fold for an outside block. Execute the outside block with your right arm as you step to your right with your right foot into a back stance. Punch across your body with your left hand to your right side.
  3. 3. Draw your left foot toward your right foot as you fold for a reinforced outside block with your fists knuckle to knuckle on your left side.
  4. 4. Step forward with your right leg as you execute the reinforced outside block, right arm.
  5. 5. Step forward with your left leg into a back stance as you fold for a cross-arm block as if to swing a baseball bat.
  6. 6. Shift to a front stance, executing a low cross-arm block.
  7. 7. Shift to a back stance, folding for a cross-arm block on your chest.
  8. 8. Execute a cross-arm high block with open hand, shifting to a front stance.
  9. 9. Roll your opponent's hand off, executing a spear-hand to the eyes, left hand, then a reverse punch to the solar plexus, right hand. KIAI
  10. 10. Look 180 degrees to your left as you fold for an inside block. Execute the inside forearm strike to your opponent's head as you grab the back of his head, then slap the inside of your left elbow with your right open hand.
  11. 11. Do an inside crescent kick to the face, right leg, while folding for a low block, left arm. Execute the low block, landing in a horse stance.
  12. 12. Look to your left. Fold your hands for an open hand strike, left hand on top. Execute the strike to your opponent's face with your left hand. Open your hand up, grabbing the back of your opponent's head, do an inside crescent kick with your right leg to your own hand, then a forearm strike to your hand.
  13. 13. Execute a reinforced back fist with your right hand to your right as you go into a cross legged stance, left leg behind your right leg.
  14. 14. Look 180 degrees to your left as you step out into a back stance with your left foot, striking your opponent with your right hand under his chin, looking away to your left.
  15. 15. Lunge forward to your left toward your opponent, crossing your legs and your hand in the air. Landing with legs crossed, execute a downward cross-arm block against your opponent's bo staff. KIAI
  16. 16. Step to your right with your right foot, executing a reinforced outside block with your right hand, stepping into a front stance with your right leg.
  17. 17. Look over your left shoulder. Turn 180 degrees to your left, executing a low, open-hand block into a back stance.
  18. 18. Thrust your hand forward, palm up. Close your fist, holding your palm down, front stance.
  19. 19. Pull your right hand back behind your head, snapping your fist into the air, then slide your left foot to your right and look at your right fist.
  20. 20. Turn 180 degrees to your left, crossing your arms and legs. Execute back fists with both your right and left hands over your shoulders.
  21. 21. Raise your right leg, doing an arm break with your right arm across your right upper leg.
  22. 22. As your right foot lands into a front stance, thrust your left hand forward toward your opponent's groin, open hand, palm up. Close your fist, rotating your palm down.
  23. 23. Draw your fist away back behind your head, lower your right hand to a low block, stabbing your fist into the air, back stance.

Return to the Junbi stance.
