Required for 4th Brown and above.

  1. 1. Junbi stance. Look to the left. Fold for a double block, both fists at your right hip, ball and socket fashion. Slide your left foot toward your left, back stance, as your execute a double knife-hand block to your left.
  2. 2. Look behind you. Fold for a double block, both fists at your left hip, ball and socket fashion. Slide your right foot into a back stance 180 degrees to your right, as you execute a double knife-hand block.
  3. 3. Look to your left 90 degrees (under your left arm). Fold for a cross-arm block at your chest. Execute the low cross-arm block as you step forward into a front stance with your left leg.
  4. 4. Fold for a reinforced outside block, right arm. Execute the reinforced outside block as you step forward into a front stance with your right foot.
  5. 5. Draw your left foot to your right foot as you fold for a bottom fist, left hand, then execute the bottom fist and a side kick, left leg, at the same time.
  6. 6. Recock your left leg as you grab the back of the opponent's head with your left hand, executing a forearm strike (against your open left hand), front stance.
  7. 7. Fold for a double block on your left side, fist ball and socket over your left hip, as you turn "dora" 180 degrees.
  8. 8. Slide the left foot up to your right foot, as you fold your right leg for a side kick and your right hand for a bottom fist. Execute the bottom fist with your right hand and side kick with your right foot at the same time. Recock your right leg as you grab the back of the opponent's head with your right hand and execute a forearm strike into your open right hand while going to a front stance.
  9. 9. Look to your left. Lower your left hand to your groin, palm out, as a block, while at the same time, putting your open right hand over your head, folding for a chop.
  10. 10. Shift on both feet, executing a knife-hand chop with your right hand to the neck of your opponent as you turn to your left, bringing your left hand over your head as a knife-hand block.
  11. 11. Do a front kick with your right leg, then a lunging back fist with your right hand. KIAI
  12. 12. Turn 225 degrees to your left, extending your left leg, back stance, Grab your shirt with both hands with crossed arms. Execute a choke hold while shifting into a front stance.
  13. 13. With your right hand, pull your opponent toward you (keeping your right elbow close to your right side) as you do a stepping front kick.
  14. 14. Step forward with your right foot into a back stance as you do a jab with your right hand, followed by a reverse punch with your left hand.
  15. 15. Turn to your right 90 degrees as you cross your hands, grabbing your opponent's collar, back stance. Execute a choke hold by pulling the opponent's collar, front stance.
  16. 16. Do a stepping front kick, left leg, as you pull your opponent into you with your left hand (keeping your right elbow close to your right side). Step forward into a back stance with a jab, left hand, followed by a reverse punch, right hand.
  17. 17. Fold for a reinforced outside block to the left, left arm, by putting your fists knuckle to knuckle over your right hip. Stepping with your left leg into a back stance, execute the reinforced outside block.
  18. 18. Fold for a reinforced outside block to the right by putting your fists knuckle to knuckle over your left hip. Execute the reinforced outside block, right arm, stepping forward with your right leg into a back stance.
  19. 19. Fold for a reinforced outside block to the left by puttin your fists knuckle to knuckle over your right hip. Execute the reinforced outside block, left arm, stepping forward with your left leg into a back stance.
  20. 20. With your left hand, grab your opponent's throat.
  21. 21. As you shift to a front stance, grab your opponent's throat with your right hand,
  22. 22. Bring your opponent's face down toward your knee as you raise your right knee into the opponent's face. KIAI
  23. 23. Turn 225 degrees to your left, executing a reinforced knife-hand block (you are now facing the same way as when you started the form).
  24. 24. Stepping forward with your right leg, turn 90 degrees to your right, executing a reinforced knife-hand block.

Return to the Junbi stance.
