Required for 7th Blue and above.

  1. 1. Begin in Junbi stance. Look left, fold for a low block, step out with left foot and execute a low block, left arm, front stance.
  2. 2. Step forward with right foot, execute reverse punch, right fist.
  3. 3. Look behind you, fold for low block, right hand, while stepping 180 degrees behind you with your right foot and execute a low block, right hand, front stance.
  4. 4. Execute outside block, right hand, back stance.
  5. 5. Look 90 degrees to your left, execute low block, left hand, front stance.
  6. 6. Knife hand block, left hand, back stance.
  7. 7. Stepping high block, right hand, back stance.
  8. 8. Stepping high block, left hand, back stance.
  9. 9. Stepping high block, right hand, back stance. KIAI
  10. 10. Look to your right, fold for low block, left hand, while stepping with your left foot and turning 270 degrees to your right by looking over your left shoulder, pivoting on the ball of your right foot, and turning to your left. Execute the low block, left hand, front stance.
  11. 11. Look behind you, fold for low block, right hand, while stepping 180 degrees behind you with your right foot, execute low block, right hand, front stance.
  12. 12. Look to your left 90 degrees, fold for a low block as you step to the left, execute a low block, left hand, front stance.
  13. 13. Stepping reverse punch, right hand, front stance.
  14. 14. Stepping reverse punch, left hand, front stance.
  15. 15. Stepping reverse punch, right hand, front stance. KIAI
  16. 16. Fold for a low knife hand block, left hand, while stepping 270 degrees around to your left with your left foot, execute low knife hand block, back stance.
  17. 17. Look 45 degrees to your right, fold for a low knife hand block, right hand, step with your right foot into a low knife hand block, right hand, back stance.
  18. 18. Look behind you over your right shoulder, fold for a low knife hand block, right hand, stepping 180 degrees behind you with your right foot, execute knife hand low block, back stance.
  19. 19. Look 45 degrees to your left, fold for a low knife hand block, left hand, as you step and execute a low knife hand block, left hand, back stance.

Return to the Junbi stance.
