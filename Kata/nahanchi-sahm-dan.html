Required for 3rd Brown and above.

Begin in a stance with heels together and toes out at a 45 degree angle, hands at your sides. Bow.

  1. 1. Bring both hands up, placing the back of the open right hand against the left palm, at about chin level. Draw your hands in toward your chest then down towards the floor, palms down, as if to strike an opponent standing in front of you just below the navel. At the same time, draw your feet together and bend your knees slightly.
  2. 2. Step with the left foot to the right, crossing in front of your right foot and folding with your right arm for an open back hand to your right. Step with the right foot into a horse stance as you execute the open back hand. Chamber your left fist to your hip as you strike.
  3. 3. Execute a left elbow smash into your right palm at a 45 degree angle to your right.
  4. 4. In a quick movement, fold both fists in a ball and socket fashion at your right hip, right hand palm up, left hand palm down. Look to your left at the same time.
  5. 5. Execute a low block with your left arm to your left.
  6. 6. Right punch to the right in a hooking fashion, while you chamber your left.
  7. 7. Fold for a left back hand strike to your left. Strike while chambering your right fist. Right elbow smash into your left palm. Ball and socket fold both fists on left hip, then execute low block with right arm to your right, and a left hooking punch to the right, chambering your right fist.
  8. 8. Cross-step to the left (your right foot crossing in front of your left foot) into a horse stance.
  9. 9. As your foot lands in the horse stance, execute an inside block motion with your right arm, and a low block motion with your left arm. Immediately reverse the positions of your arms.
  10. 10. Place the back of your right fist under your left elbow. Draw your left knee up, sweeping the left foot towards the right knee until your toes touch the inside of your right leg. As your left foot lands back into a horse stance, strike to your left, twisting your left fist over for a bottom fist.
  11. 11. Draw your right knee up, sweeping the right foot towards the left knee until your toes touch the inside of your left leg. At the same moment, strike to your right, twisting your left fist over for a bottom fist strike to the right.
  12. 12. Chamber both fists quickly to your right hip, ball and socket fashion, looking quickly to your left as you do so. Thrust both fists to the left, your right arm moving into a short hooking motion punch and your left arm moving in a bottom fist motion straight to your left. KIAI
  13. 13. Fold for and execute a left hand open hand strike to your left; chamber your right fist to your right hip at the same time.
  14. 14. Right elbow smash into your left palm at a 45 degree angle to your left.
  15. 15. In a quick movement, chamber both fists on your left hip ball and socket fashion, while at the same time looking to the right.
  16. 16. Low block with your right arm; hooking punch to the right with your left arm.
  17. 17. Step with the left foot to the right, crossing in front of your right foot, leaving your arms in position. As you land in the horse stance, execute an inside block motion with your left arm, and a low block motion with your right arm. Quickly reverse the positions of your arms.
  18. 18. Bring your left fist up under your right elbow, palm down.
  19. 19. Draw your right knee up, sweeping the left foot towards the right knee until your toes touch the inside of your right leg. At the same moment, strike to your right, twisting your fist over for a bottom fist strike to your right.
  20. 20. Draw your left knee up, sweeping the left foot towards the right knee until your toes touch the inside of your right leg. At the same moment, strike to your left, twisting your fist over for a bottom fist strike to your left.
  21. 21. Chamber both fists quickly to your left hip, ball and socket fashion, looking quickly to your right as you do so. Thrust both fists to the right, your left arm moving in a short hooking motion and your right arm moving in a bottom fist motion straight to your right. KIAI

Return to the Junbi stance.

Bring both hands up, placing the back of the right open hand against the left pam at about chin level, as you draw your right foot up against your left foot, knees slightly bent. Draw the hands in toward your chest then down towards the floor, palms down, as if to strike an opponent standing in front of you just below the navel. Snap into a junbi stance, then bring your feet together and hands to your sides. Bow.
