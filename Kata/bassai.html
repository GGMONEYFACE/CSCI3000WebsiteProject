Required for 1st Black and above.

  1. 1. Junbi stance. Raise your left foot knee high as you bring your right hand toward your hips, folding into a fist and bring your left hand in front of your right hand.
  2. 2. Lower your left foot beside your right foot as you lower your hands. Extend both arms completely out in front of your waist, clasping your right hand around your left thumb.
  3. 3. Pivot 180 degrees to your left, extending both your arms straight back with your hands clasped. Lunging forward, execute a reinforced outside block crossing your left leg behind your right.
  4. 4. Turn 180 degrees to your left, executing an outside block with your left arm into a front stance. Then execute an outside block with your right arm.
  5. 5. Folding with your left hand for an inside block, turn 180 degrees to your right, and execute an inside block into a front stance. Execute a right hand outside block.
  6. 6. Look 90 degrees to your right. Bring your right foot knee high as your execute an arm break with your right hand. Folding with your right hand for an inside block, turn 90 degrees to your right executing an inside block, front stance.
  7. 7. Execute an outside block with your left hand.
  8. 8. Look to your left. Turning 90 degrees to your left, shift your left foot into a horse stance as you bring your left hand in front of your chest into an open knife-hand block (palm down).
  9. 9. Execute a spear hand toward the throat of your opponent with your left hand, followed by a reverse punch with your right hand. Execute an outside block with your right hand, snapping your hips to your left.
  10. 10. Execute a reverse punch with your left hand, shifting into a horse stance followed by an outside block with your left hand, shifting your hips to your right.
  11. 11. Fold for a reinforced chop over your left shoulder as you step with your left leg. Execute the reinforced chop as you step with your right leg.
  12. 12. Fold for a reinforced chop over your right shoulder. Execute the reinforced chop as you step forward with your left foot.
  13. 13. Fold for a reinforced chop over your left shoulder. Execute the reinforced chop as you step forward with your right foot.
  14. 14. As you step back with your right foot, execute a knife-hand resistance block with your left hand bringing your right hand to your hip.
  15. 15. Pivoting 180 degrees to your left, bring your right foot directly behind your left foot executing a double knife-hand block over your head. Execute a side kick with your right leg, and a bottom fist with your right hand at the same time. KIAI
  16. 16. Turning 180 degrees, recock the right leg and execute a reinforced knife-hand chop in a back stance.
  17. 17. Execute a reinforced knife-hand chop as you step forward with your right foot into a back stance.
  18. 18. Slide your right foot back to your left foot as you thrust both hands to your groin to protect yourself.
  19. 19. Raise both your fists, crossing at the knuckles until they are above your head.
  20. 20. Step forward with your right foot stomping the ground and landing in a front stance, as you execute two bottom fists to the ribs (at the same time).
  21. 21. Execute a lunging double strike, first the left hand with a spear hand, then the right hand with a punch. KIAI
  22. 22. Turn 180 degrees to your left and fold for and execute a low knife-hand block.
  23. 23. Thrust your right hand forward open palm up as you shift into a front stance, folding your left arm toward your shoulder. Closing your fist and turning it over, pull your right hand behind your head executing an uppercut. At the same time, execute a left hand low block.
  24. 24. Step forward into a front stance, executing an inside block with your right arm striking your left palm.
  25. 25. Execute a stepping inside crescent kick with your right leg turning 90 degrees to the left and landing in a horse stance. At the same time, strike with a low bottom fist (with your right hand) to the groin.
  26. 26. Fold both hands at your chest. Next, extending your left hand 90 degrees to your left, grab your opponent's head.
  27. 27. Execute a stepping inside crescent kick with your right leg, turning 180 degrees and striking your right foot on your left hand, then your right forearm on your left hand.
  28. 28. Execute two punches toward the floor, lowering your right hand, then raising it back up over your left hand and striking again.
  29. 29. Look 90 degrees to your right. Slide your right foot back to your left foot. Folding for a double punch on your left hip, step 90 degrees to the right into a front stance with your right foot. Execute a double punch, left hand to the face, right hand to the stomach.
  30. 30. Slide your right foot back as you fold both hands on your left hip for another double punch, bringing your hands to the right hip.
  31. 31. Take a small step forward with your right foot and execute an inside crescent kick with your left leg. Deliver a double punch, left hand to the face, right hand to the stomach.
  32. 32. Slide your left foot back to your right side. Fold both hands to your right hip.
  33. 33. Fold for a double punch, bringing your hands to the left hip. Take a small step forward with your left foot, and execute an inside crescent kick with your right foot, landing in a front stance. Execute a double punch, left hand to the face and right hand low to the stomach.
  34. 34. Turn to your left 180 degrees, striking with a back fist toward the floor, then turn 90 degrees to your right and strike with a back fist toward the floor.
  35. 35. Fold over your left shoulder for a reinforced chop as you step forward with your left foot. Execute the reinforced chop as you step forward with your right foot.
  36. 36. Turn 180 degrees to your right, folding over your left shoulder for a reinforced chop. Step with your right foot, executing the reinforced chop to your right.
  37. 37. Look 180 degrees to your left. As you step forward with your right foot crossing over your left foot, fold over your right shoulder for a reinforced chop. Stepping forward with your left foot, execute a reinforced chop into a back stance.

Return to ready position and then return to the Junbi stance.
