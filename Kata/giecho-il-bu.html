Required for 10th Gold and above.

  1. 1. Junbi (ready) stance. Look to your left. Fold with your left hand for a low block while stepping to your left with your left foot. Turn 90 degrees to your left into a front stance executing a low block with left arm.
  2. 2. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your right hand.
  3. 3. Look over your right shoulder. Fold for a low block with your right hand while stepping behind with your right foot. Turn 180 degrees to the right into a front stance, executing a low block with right arm.
  4. 4. Step forward with your left leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your left hand.
  5. 5. Look to your left. Fold for a low block with your left hand while stepping to your left with your left foot. At the same time turn 90 degrees to your left into a front stance, executing a low block with left arm.
  6. 6. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your right hand.
  7. 7. Step forward with your left leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your left hand.
  8. 8. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your right hand, and KIAI.
  9. 9. Look to your right. Fold for a low block with your left hand while spinning on your right foot turning 270 degrees back and around with your left foot into a front stance. Executing a low block with your left arm.
  10. 10. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your right hand.
  11. 11. Look over your right shoulder. Fold for a low block with your right hand while stepping behind with your right foot. At the same time, turn 180 degrees to your right into a front stance, executing a low block with right arm.
  12. 12. Step forward with your left leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your left hand.
  13. 13. Look to your left. Fold for a low block with your left hand while stepping to your left with your left foot. At the same time turn on your right foot turn 90 degrees to your left front stance, executing a low block with left arm.
  14. 14. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your right hand.
  15. 15. Step forward with your left leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your left hand.
  16. 16. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your right hand, and KIAI.
  17. 17. Look to your right. Fold for a low block with your left hand while spinning on your right foot, turning 270 degrees back and around with your left foot into a front stance. At the same time execute a low block with left arm.
  18. 18. Step forward with your right leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your right hand.
  19. 19. Look over your right shoulder. Fold for a low block with your right hand while stepping behind with your right foot. At the same time turn 180 degrees to your right foot into a front stance, executing a low block with right arm.
  20. 20. Step forward with your left leg into a front stance while executing a reverse punch to the solar plexus with your left hand.

Return to the junbi stance.
