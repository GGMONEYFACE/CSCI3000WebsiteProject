Required for 1st Red and above.

  1. 1. From junbi stance, bring your right foot to your left foot while chambering your left hand, palm open facing your body, with your right fist against your left palm, fingers toward body.
  2. 2. Looking to your right, step outward to the left with your left foot, immediately kneeling on your right knee while executing a downward strike, right fist, while punching across the chest with your left fist, fingers upward, left fist against upper right arm.
  3. 3. While still looking right, stand up into horse stance, chambering both fists on left hip, left fist fingers up, right fist fingers inward with right fist above left fist.
  4. 4. Do not step, but pivot on the balls of your feet and execute a right fist low block 90 degrees to your right, front stance.
  5. 5. Look left 90 degrees (back to front). Pivot on the balls of both feet back into horse stance while chambering right fist on right hip and punching across your chest with the left fist, fingers down.
  6. 6. Step forward with left leg, executing a low block, left fist, front stance.
  7. 7. Execute an upward movement, right fist, punching to the chin of opponent from below, chamber left fist.
  8. 8. Open right fist then close it grabbing your opponent with your right hand, pulling him toward you stopping your right fist on your upper left arm. At the same time you are jumping forward, lifting your right knee high (using your lower right leg to protect your groin), then landing on your right foot while placing left foot behind your right foot. Your left fist punches down into opponent's lower abdomen.
  9. 9. Step back with left foot into a deep back stance while slowly moving into a low block with right fist. (With this movement, you are breaking your opponent's grip on your left forearm.)
  10. 10. Look behind you, turn 180 degrees, fold for and execute a low block, left fist, front stance, left foot forward.
  11. 11. Execute an upward movement, right fist, punching to the chin from below.
  12. 12. Open right fist then close it grabbing your opponent with your right hand, pulling him toward you stopping your right fist on your upper left arm. At the same time you are jumping forward, lifting your right knee high (using your lower right leg to protect your groin), then landing on your right foot while placing left foot behind your right foot. Your left fist punches down into opponent's lower abdomen.
  13. 13. Step back with left foot into a deep back stance while slowly moving into a low block with right fist.
  14. 14. Look behind you, turn 180 degrees, fold for and execute a low block, left fist, front stance, left foot forward.
  15. 15. Without moving the right foot, swing your open left hand (fingers pointing straight up and palm to your right) and your left foot to the left into a horse stance. (Keep your eyes on your left hand.) Strike the left palm with the right forearm so that the tips of the fingers of the left hand come to the level of the right wrist, and at the same time place your right foot (instep) against the back of your left knee. KIAI
  16. 16. Move your right foot to the right into a horse stance while moving the left open hand (palm down) under your right upper arm and swinging your right arm in a large clockwise circle past your forehead up then down to the right, then left (to entrap opponent's arm), then execute a knife hand block, left hand, chambering right fist to right hip.
  17. 17. Double punch, right and then left hands.
  18. 18. Look left, step out with left foot into a front stance, low block, left fist.
  19. 19. High reverse punch, right fist.
  20. 20. Step forward with right foot into a back stance, executing a right knife hand block.
  21. 21. Your feet switch places, pulling your right foot back, then stepping forward with your left foot into a left hand knife hand block.
  22. 22. Reverse punch, right fist, front stance.
  23. 23. Step forward with right foot into a back stance, right hand knife hand block.
  24. 24. Look behind you, turning 180 degrees (to the left) into a front stance, left foot forward, left fist low block.
  25. 25. Execute an upward movement, right fist, punching to the chin of opponent from below, chamber left fist.
  26. 26. Open right fist then close it grabbing your opponent with your right hand, pulling him toward you stopping your right fist on your upper left arm. At the same time you are jumping forward, lifting your right knee high (using your lower right leg to protect your groin), then landing on your right foot while placing left foot behind your right foot. Your left fist punches down into opponent's lower abdomen.
  27. 27. Step back with left foot into a deep back stance while slowly moving into a low block with right fist. (With this movement, you are breaking your opponent's grip on your left forearm.)
  28. 28. Look behind you, turn 180 degrees, fold for and execute a low block, left fist, front stance, left foot forward.
  29. 29. Look to your right. Keep your feet in place, chamber left fist to left hip while punching open right hand to your right, first down then upward into a middle level block, palm up.
  30. 30. Step forward with your right foot into a front stance while swinging both arms in large, clockwise circular motions, ending in an upward pushing open hand block with the right hand and a downward open hand pushing block with your left hand.
  31. 31. Step forward with your left foot into a front stance while changing positions of your hands, ending in an upward pushing open hand block with the left hand and a downward open hand pushing block with your right hand.
  32. 32. Step forward with your right foot and change positions of your hands again, ending in an upward pushing open hand block with the right hand and a downward open hand pushing block with your left hand.
  33. 33. Keeping feet in place, execute a low block with the right hand, while going into a back stance.
  34. 34. Lunge forward with both feet while positioning open hands out in front of you, left hand just above forehead level, right hand about waist level, left thumb point left, right thumb pointing right, as if grabbing a large vertical pipe. (This sets you up to block an attack with your left hand and grab the opponent between the legs with your right hand. In the next step, you are throwing your opponent behind you, using his own momentum against him.)
  35. 35. Leap backward, turning 360 degrees in the air, lifting both feet as high as possible, landing with both feet at the same time in a right foot forward back stance, right hand knife hand block.
  36. 36. Step back with your right foot into a back stance, left hand knife hand block.

Slide your left foot back to your right foot and assume the form's ready position: Both hands on left hip, right fist in left palm with fingers of right fist toward your body. Return to the Junbi stance.
