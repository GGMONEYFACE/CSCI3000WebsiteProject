Basic One-Steps

1.

Step to left with left foot, execute knife-hand block with right hand, grab wrist, then execute spinning round kick with right foot. KIAI

2.

Step in with left foot into a back stance, execute a high block with left arm and then a reverse punch to the solar plexus with right fist. KIAI

3.

Step in with right foot into a back stance and execute an inside block with right arm. Slide forward and strike opponent with right elbow. KIAI.

4.

Step in with right foot into a front stance while executing an outside block with left arm. Execute a reverse palm heel strike with right hand to opponent's left cheek. KIAI

5.

Defense against kick: Step to outside with left foot while executing a low block with right arm. Follow with a reverse punch to the face with left fist. KIAI

Intermediate One-Steps

6.

Step to the left while executing a knife-hand block with the right hand. Execute a spinning round kick to the mid section with the right leg, re-cock kicking leg and execute a side kick to the outside of the opponent's right knee. Landing in a front stance, execute a reverse punch with your left hand behind opponent's right ear. KIAI

7.

Step in with left leg assuming a back stance while executing a high block with your left arm. Strike the opponent with a reverse punch in the solar plexus, right hand, front stance. Your left hand turns palm up to grab opponent's hand. Step through to the left with your right leg to bring opponent's punching arm up onto your right shoulder to break elbow. Spin your upper body to the left to strike your opponent with your left elbow to the ribs. Slide right foot back to sweep your opponent's right leg. Drop to your left knee, keeping opponent's arm in an arm lock, reverse punch to the face or ribs. KIAI

8.

Step in with right foot into a back stance and execute an inside block with right arm. Slide forward and strike opponent with right elbow. Loop your right arm over opponent's neck and execute inverted head lock, grabbing opponent's chin with your right hand and putting your left hand on the back of his head. Step back with right foot, back stance, twist hips to the right into a front stance while pulling right hand up, pushing left hand down, snapping opponent's neck. KIAI

9.

Step in with left foot into a front stance while executing an outside block with left arm. Step forward with right leg, lunging into opponent with a reverse palm heel to the face with your right hand. KIAI

10.

Defense against kick: Step left, execute low block with right arm and catch kick with right arm. Stepping front kick with left foot on the inside of opponent's left thigh. As opponent falls on his back, follow through with a heel strike (axe kick or stomp) to the sternum. KIAI

Advanced One-Steps

11.

Step at a 45-degree angle to the left while performing a knife-hand block with the right hand. Execute a spinning round kick with your right leg to your opponent's right inside thigh. Wrist lock attacker's punching arm with your right hand, holding his hand against your chest. Strike opponent's triceps's pressure point with your left elbow. As opponent goes down, strike with a right hand reverse punch behind opponent's ear. Grab hair with right hand. Place left hand in small of back while stepping back with right leg. Pull with right hand and push with left hand at the same time, causing opponent to fall to the ground. Drive right heel into the sternum. KIAI

12.

Step in left leg and execute a left arm high block, then an upper cut to solar plexus with your right fist. Slide right foot up to your left foot, executing right elbow strike under the chin while grabbing the punching arm with left hand. Right hand grabs into head lock and spin to the left (both partners now back to back) execute a neck break by dropping to your right knee while pulling your opponent's head downward over your right shoulder. KIAI

13.

Step in right leg into a back stance. Right hand inside parry block execute a spinning hook kick with left leg. Follow up with reverse punch with right hand. Step back a safe distance. While stepping back, shift weight on back leg and execute a side kick left leg. Right hand reverse punch to the belt line, with your arm at a 45 degree downward angle. KIAI

14.

Step with right leg to the right. Left hand outside block. Standing side kick with left leg. Jump spinning back kick with right leg. Land with a reverse punch with your left hand and follow up with a right ridge hand. KIAI

15.

(Attacker using a spinning round kick) Step through inside crescent with the right leg stopping the round kick follow through with spinning outside crescent with left leg. Execute a spinning round kick with the right leg to the pressure point in the thigh muscle (side of the opponent's right leg). Dash to the right (45 degree angle) with a left forearm strike to the neck. After opponent falls, execute a heel strike with left foot. KIAI

Note: In the advanced one-steps, not all areas to strike on the opponent are indicated. You strike where you see an opening at that moment.
