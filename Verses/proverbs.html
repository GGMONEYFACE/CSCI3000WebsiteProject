Proverbs 22:6

Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it. (ESV)

Reflection for Martial Artists

This verse emphasizes the value of early discipline and spiritual mentoring. In training, we aim to build consistency and godly character that lasts beyond the dojo.

Action

  • Practice a skill today and teach it to someone else.
  • Encourage a younger student with scriptural truth.
