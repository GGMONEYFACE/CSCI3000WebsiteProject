Philippians 4:13

I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me. (KJV)

Reflection for Martial Artists

Victory comes through reliance on Christ, not self. Frustration fades when we focus on Christ’s strength in every challenge.

Action

  • Pray before practice for spiritual strength.
  • Choose one tough goal and trust God through it.
