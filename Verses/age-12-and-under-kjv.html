Joshua 1:9

Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be frightened, and do not be dismayed, for the Lord your God is with you wherever you go. (ESV)

Reflection for Martial Artists

God’s presence is your source of courage. Do not battle in fear; obey and step forward in confidence.

Action

  • Identify one fear in training and face it with prayer and scripture.
  • Team up with a classmate to encourage each other in boldness.
