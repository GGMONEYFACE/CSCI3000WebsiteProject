Required for 8th Orange and above.

  1. 1. Junbi stance. Look to your left and fold for an outside block with your left hand while stepping left with left foot. Turn 90 degrees to your left into a back stance, executing an outside block with the left arm.
  2. 2. Turn left arm into punching position as if aiming at attacker. Execute a stepping reverse punch with right hand, right foot forward, front stance.
  3. 3. Look over your right shoulder, fold for an outside block with your right hand, step behind you with your right foot, spinning on your left foot and turning 180 degrees into a back stance, executing an outside block with right arm.
  4. 4. Turn right arm into punching position as if aiming at attacker. Execute a reverse punch with left hand, left foot forward, front stance.
  5. 5. Look to your left, fold for a low block with your left hand and step to your left with your left foot, spinning on your right foot, turn 90 degrees to your left into a front stance, left foot forward, and execute a low block with left arm.
  6. 6. Execute a stepping side punch with the right hand to the ribs, stepping with the right foot and landing in a horse stance.
  7. 7. Fold for a left hand spinning bottom fist, left hand on top. Turn 180 degrees, spinning on your right foot with your left foot going behind your right foot and landing in a horse stance with your left foot forward, executing a spinning bottom fist with your left hand to the head.
  8. 8. Spin once again on your forward foot (left foot) to land in a horse stance right foot forward and execute a stepping side punch with the right hand to the ribs. KIAI
  9. 9. Look to your right, fold for an outside block with the left hand, stepping back and around with your left foot turn 270 degrees to your left into a back stance, executing an outside block with your left arm.
  10. 10. Turn left arm into punching position as if aiming at attacker. Execute a reverse punch with right hand, right foot forward, front stance.
  11. 11. Look over your right shoulder, fold for an outside block with your right hand, step behind you with your right foot, spinning on your left foot and turning 180 degrees into a back stance, executing an outside block with right arm.
  12. 12. Turn right arm into punching position as if aiming at attacker. Execute a reverse punch with left hand, left foot forward, front stance.
  13. 13. Look to your left, fold for a low block with your left hand and step to your left with your left foot, spinning on your right foot, turn 90 degrees to your left into a front stance, left foot forward, and execute a low block with left arm.
  14. 14. Execute a stepping side punch with the right hand to the ribs, stepping with the right foot and landing in a horse stance.
  15. 15. Fold for a left hand spinning bottom fist, left hand on top. Turn 180 degrees, spinning on your right foot and landing in a horse stance with your left foot forward, executing a spinning bottom fist to the head.
  16. 16. Spin once again on your forward foot (left foot) to land in a horse stance right foot forward and execute a stepping side punch with the right hand to the ribs. KIAI
  17. 17. Look to your right, fold for an outside block with the left hand, stepping back around with your left foot turn 270 degrees to your left into a back stance, executing an outside block with your left arm.
  18. 18. Turn left arm into punching position as if aiming at attacker. Execute a reverse punch with right hand, right foot forward, front stance.
  19. 19. Look over your right shoulder, fold for an outside block with your right hand, step behind you with your right foot, spinning on your left foot and turning 180 degrees into a back stance, executing an outside block with right arm.
  20. 20. Turn right arm into punching position as if aiming at attacker. Execute a reverse punch with left hand, left foot forward, front stance.

Return to the Junbi stance.
